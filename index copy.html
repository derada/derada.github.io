Sue deRada is a registered nurse, licensed bodyworker, and certified grief counselor. Sue’s practice goal is to enhance health and healing through prevention and intervention. “The client leads the practitioner to discover imbalances, fine-tune and create equilibrium in the subtle interactions of body, mind, spirit.” This includes self-care and professional coaching. Together you develop an individualized blend of practices like mindfulness and awareness to balance bodily aspects like nutrition, touch, yoga movement-exercise, balancing work & play with mind & spirit aspects like practicing gratitude, meditation/prayer, joy. We are like our universe- the totality of space-time, matter, energy and everything that exists therein; down to the smallest subatomic particles.
